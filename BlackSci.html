NAME: Marie Van Brittan Brown
NOTED FOR: Building the home security system, modern day CCTV.
ABOUT: Born October 30 1922, Marie worked as a nurse in New York where she lived with her husband. Their work schedules were such that they were not at home together at certain times. During these times alone at home, Marie was concerned about her safety. She,together with her husband, designed what would become the basis for home surveillance technology.

NAME: Katherine Johnson
NOTED FOR: Her calculations which sent the first American into space and the first American to orbit the surface of the earth.
ABOUT: Katherine was born on 26th August,1918 and lived to be 101 years old. She lived in a time where desegregation of blacks and whites was gradually happening. Obtaining her first degree in Math and French at age 18, she was one of the first 3 black people to attend graduate school in West Virginia University. She joined NASA and was part of an elite black women's team known as computers.

NAME: Lewis H. Latimer
NOTED FOR: Replacing the ephemeral paper filaments with the durable carbon filament to allow the light bulb shine continuously.
ABOUT: Born in the United States on September 4,1848, Lewis Latimer at age 15/16 was enlisted to join the US Navy during its Civil War that abolished slavery.
Latimer joined the U.S. Electric Lighting Company where he perfected the light bulb. Thomas Edison may have invented the light bulb, but Lewis Latimer made it better.
